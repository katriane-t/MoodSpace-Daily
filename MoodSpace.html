Colour psychology is the study of hues as a determinant of human behavior. Colour influences perceptions, and have qualities that can cause certain emotions in people. How color influences individuals may differ depending on age, gender, and culture. Although color associations can vary contextually between cultures, color preference is relatively uniform across gender and race.
